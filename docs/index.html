HOW IT WORKS

THREE
STEPS

01
PASTE YOUR SCRIPT
Drop in your text, or let the AI summarizer condense it to punchy bullet points. Adjust font, size, alignment, line spacing.
02
HIT LAUNCH
The window floats above everything. Invisible to Zoom, Google Meet, OBS, and Loom — nobody on your call sees it.
03
PRESS SPACE
Smooth auto-scroll starts. Speed up with ↑, slow down with ↓. Hit T for pass-through — the window disappears to your mouse completely.
FEATURES

BUILT FOR
CREATORS

🔒
SCREEN SHARE INVISIBLE
Uses OS-level APIs — NSWindowSharingNone on macOS, SetWindowDisplayAffinity on Windows. Not a CSS trick. Actually invisible.
OS-LEVEL API
🪟
ALWAYS ON TOP
Floats above every application. Joins all macOS spaces so it follows you between desktops. Never loses its position.
👁
OPACITY CONTROL
Slide from 10% to 100%. See your slides or camera feed through the teleprompter. Works on both platforms without the broken OS API.
🖱
PASS-THROUGH MODE
Press T and your mouse falls right through the window. Click on whatever's behind it. Keyboard shortcuts still work.
🪞
MIRROR MODE
Flip text horizontally for physical teleprompter glass setups. Beam splitter ready.
✦
AI SUMMARIZER
Paste a wall of text, hit the AI button, get back punchy bullet points. Powered by Claude. Reads faster, lands harder.
